How to Measure

🟢 Chest Measurement

Measure around the fullest part of your chest, keeping the tape horizontal.

🟢 Length

Measure from the highest point of the shoulder to the bottom hem.

🟢 Sleeve Length

Measure from the shoulder seam to the end of the sleeve.

🟢 Shoulder

Measure from one shoulder seam to the other across the back.

Size Selection Tips

  • Measure yourself before ordering
  • Our Oversize fit is designed to be loose - if you prefer fitted, consider sizing down
  • Allow 1-2 inches tolerance for comfort
  • Check the measurement table against your existing well-fitting t-shirt
  • In case of doubt between sizes, go for the larger size for oversize look
