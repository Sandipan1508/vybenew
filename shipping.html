Shipping Policy

Information about delivery and shipping

Shipping Rates

  • Free Shipping: Available on all orders above ₹999
  • Standard Shipping: ₹99 for orders below ₹999
  • Express Delivery: ₹199 (Delivery in 2-3 business days) - Available in select cities

Delivery Time

  • Standard Delivery: 5-7 business days
  • Express Delivery: 2-3 business days
  • Orders are processed within 24-48 hours of confirmation
  • Delivery times may vary during festive seasons or due to unforeseen circumstances

Shipping Partners

We partner with reliable courier services to ensure safe and timely delivery of your orders. You will receive tracking details via email and SMS once your order is dispatched.

Order Tracking

You can track your order using the tracking link sent to your registered email and phone number. Alternatively, visit our Track Order page and enter your Order ID to check the status.

Delivery Areas

We deliver to all major cities and towns across India. For remote locations, delivery may take additional 2-3 days. Currently, we do not offer international shipping.

Shipping Address

Please provide complete delivery address including landmark and phone number for smooth delivery. Our delivery partner may contact you for confirmation before delivery.

Order Changes

Once an order is placed, we cannot modify the shipping address. Please ensure correct address at the time of purchase. Contact us immediately if you notice any errors.

Failed Delivery Attempt

If delivery fails due to incorrect address or unavailability of recipient, our delivery partner will attempt 2 more times. After failed attempts, the order will be returned to us. You may need to place a new order or contact customer support for assistance.
