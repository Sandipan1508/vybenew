Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about VybeCulture

What is your return policy?
We offer a 30-day return policy for all unused items in original condition. Simply contact us to initiate a return. Once we receive and inspect your item, we'll process your refund within 5-7 business days.
How do I track my order?
You can track your order using the Order ID sent to your email. Visit our Track Order page and enter your Order ID to see the current status of your delivery.
What are the shipping charges?
We offer FREE shipping on orders above ₹999. For orders below ₹999, a flat shipping fee of ₹99 will be charged. Delivery typically takes 5-7 business days.
How do I change my delivery address?
If your order hasn't been shipped yet, please contact us immediately with your Order ID and updated address. We'll try our best to accommodate your request.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit/debit cards, UPI, and digital wallets. All transactions are secure and encrypted.
How do I contact customer support?
You can reach us via email at vybeculture2026@gmail.com or call us at +91 7982504396. We're available Monday to Saturday, 9AM to 8PM.
Do you offer size exchanges?
Yes! If you need a different size, simply initiate a return for your current item and place a new order for the desired size. We recommend checking our size guide before ordering.
How can I cancel my order?
You can cancel your order within 24 hours of placing it, provided it hasn't been shipped. Contact us with your Order ID to request cancellation.
